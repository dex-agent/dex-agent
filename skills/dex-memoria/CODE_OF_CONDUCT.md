# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to keep this project respectful, practical, and safe for contributors.
Disagreement is welcome when it improves the work; harassment, personal attacks,
or sharing private data is not.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear, kind, and specific.
- Keep review feedback about the work.
- Protect private data and secrets.
- Assume examples and reports may become public.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Posting secrets, tokens, real operational memory, or sensitive logs.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers ask to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when needed to protect the project and contributors.

Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md` instead of public issue
threads.
